BEAUTIFUL PLACES TO VISIT IN: UTTARAKHAND


Uttarakhand, popularly known as 'DEVBHOOMI' or 'The land of GOD' with its lively natural beauty, fascinating mountains, pure air and water attracts the innumerable crowd from across the world. Let's explore 5 places of this states.

ABOUT

Uttarakhand is one of the most beautiful states of India. Snow-capped Himalayan peaks, serpentine rivers, revered temples, quaint villages, vibrant culture, and World Heritage Sites, Uttarakhand's natural beauty and diversity attract travelers from all over the world.
Be it river rafting in the Ganges, trekking to some of the fascinating peaks, skiing through some of the picturesque slopes, enjoying a cable-car ride, Uttarakhand is a dream destination for all.
Here are Top 5 places to visit in Uttarakhand.

NAINITAL

Nainital is a Himalayan resort town in the Kumaon region of Uttarakhand. Formerly a British hill station, it sets around Nainital Lake, a popular boating site with a Hindu Temple "Naina Devi Temple" on its north shore. Nainital offers everthing required for a perfect busy holiday. Plenty of hotels and resorts are set on the hillside, the Mall Road is a busy shopping hub, cafes and restaurants serve both local and global cuisine. People can visit and enjoy various places in Nainital such as: Naini Lake, Nainital Zoo, Naina Devi Temple, Raj Bhawan, Tiffin Top Point, etc.

ALMORA

Almora: The Cultural Capital of the Kumaon region got its name from a plant named Kilmora which is commonly found here. The best time to visit Almora is from March to May and mid-September to December. One of the most recognized temple is 'Jageshwar Dham' which is in the Almora District. There are various Temples in Almora which are a must to visit including Jageshwar Dham if you are planning to visit Almora. Few of them are:Nanda Devi Temple, Chitai Golu Devta Temple, Katarmal Sun Temple, etc.

KAUSANI

Kausani is a hill station and Village situated in Bageshwar district Kumaon region in the state of Uttarakhand, India. It is famous for its scenic splendour and its spectacular 300 km-wide panoramic view of Himalayan peaks like Trisul, Nanda Devi and Panchchuli. Mahatma Gandhi called this place the 'Switzerland of India' due to similarity in landscapes. Kausani offers one of the most beautiful views of Sunrise and Sunset. If you are looking for mental peace, exotic view and fresh air all at one place then Kausani is that place. Places to explore in Kausani are Anasakti Ashram, Kausani Tea Estate, Baijnath Temple, Someshwar Temple, etc.

RANIKHET

Ranikhet is a hill station and cantonment town, near Almora Town in Almora district in the Indian state of Uttarakhand. Ranikhet comes in the Kumaon region of Uttarakhand. It is known for its views of the Himalayas. Hundreds of bells adorn Jhula Devi Temple, dedicated to the Hindu goddess Durga and tranquility prevails in the Haidakhan Baba Temple in Ranikhet. If you are a nature lover and want mental peace then Ranikhet is a must for you to visit atleast once.

LANSDOWNE

Lansdowne is a hill station in the north Indian state of Uttarakhand. It is situated in the Garhwal region. Lansdowne is a quiet and calm town with the old-world colonial charm which is still intact after so many years. Lansdowne offers a full vacation pack and has so many places which you can visit during your visit to this serene place. Top attractions in Lansdowne are Tarkeshwar Mahadev Temple, Bhulla Taal Lake, Tip-in-Top Point, St. Mary's Church, St. John's Church, Santoshi Mata Temple, Durga Devi Temple. The most beautiful part of Lansdowne is that it is home to the brave Garhwal Rifles Regiment. You can experience the stories of great valour of the soldiers of India by visiting the 'Darwan Singh Museum' also known as Garhwal Rifles Museum. Except for the monsoon season, Lansdowne is a tourist destination that is flocked with tourists year-round and you can easily get the view of the panoramic sight of the mountains, flora and fauna.
